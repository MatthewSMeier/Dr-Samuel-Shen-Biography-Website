PROFESSIONAL INTERESTS

Sampling error estimate for climate data, uncertainty quantification for climate change assessment, methods for detecting climate changes, software development for big climate data visualization and delivery, climate observation network designs, stochastic climate models, seasonal forecasting of precipitation, analysis of remote sensing data, agroclimatic data analysis, spatial statistics applications, data analysis methods for nonlinear and non-stationary processes, fluid dynamics, and nonlinear waves.

RESEARCH IN PROGRESS

  1. Climate data analysis, visualization and delivery (in collaboration with Scripps Institution of Oceanography/ UCSD, University of Maryland, UCLA, NASA Jet Propulsion Lab, and NOAA National Climatic Data Center): Software development for big climate data visualization and delivery, uncertainty quantification of global precipitation, advanced statistical methods for estimating cloud parameters, multivariate regression for forecasting, Bayesian OA algorithms, climate models, climate data compression, nonlinear and non-stationary data analysis, detection of climate changes, reconstruction of climate data, design of optimal observation systems, changes of ocean heat contents, agroclimatic data analysis, and ground validation for satellite observations.
